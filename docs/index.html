How it works

  1. Open QuickSort
  2. Select a folder (Downloads, Desktop, etc.)
  3. Click Organize

Security & privacy

QuickSort runs completely offline. Your files never leave your computer.

macOS: Right-click → Open → Open (first time only) Windows: Click “More info” → “Run anyway”
